What is KAA?:
Kelas Aliran Agama (KAA) or Religious Stream Class (Arabic: الفصل الديني)
is a type of institutional group of education established and managed by the Malaysian Ministry of Education (MOE).
[1] KAA forms Religious Education Institution (IPA) with two other types of institutional group of education,
which are National Islamic Secondary School (SMKA) and Government-funded Religious School (SABK).
